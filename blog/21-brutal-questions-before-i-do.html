21 Brutal Questions to Ask Before You Say “I Do”

Love is a promise. Marriage is a plan. These questions won’t ruin romance—they protect it.

The Questions

  1. How do we fight—and how do we end a fight?
  2. What counts as “cheating” to you?
  3. Who handles money? What’s our monthly plan?
  4. Kids: when, how many, and what if it’s hard?
  5. What does faith look like in our week?
  6. Where’s the line between privacy and secrecy?
  7. Whose family has the loudest vote in our house?
  8. What’s our boundary with work after 7 p.m.?
  9. How do we support mental health—yours and mine?
  10. What holidays matter, and where do we spend them?
  11. What do we owe our pasts—and to whom?
  12. What’s our rule about exes and “old friends”?
  13. What does intimacy mean when life gets busy?
  14. How do we handle debt, savings, and giving?
  15. What’s non-negotiable for your health and mine?
  16. How do we apologize—and what makes it real?
  17. Who do we become under stress?
  18. What’s one dream you won’t sacrifice?
  19. What are we building in five years?
  20. What fear are you afraid to tell me?
  21. What promise can I actually hold you to?
