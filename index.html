About the Author

Corey L. Johnson

Corey L. Johnson's love for storytelling began in Ripley, Tennessee, near the childhood home of literary legend Alex Haley. After years in the corporate world, Corey returned to his passion for writing, crafting emotionally resonant stories about life, love, faith, and the decisions that define us.
