Ain't No Going Back Coming Soon

Frazier James’s life is a complete lie. For 28 days, he’s been pretending to go to a job he lost, lying straight to his wife Clara’s face every single morning. Their marriage is already struggling to survive after an old tragedy, and his deceit is making things worse. Just when he thinks it can’t get any messier, a letter arrives. An old college flame has died. And she’s left him a 14-year-old daughter he never knew existed. The catch? He only gets custody if he moves this angry, grieving teenager into a crumbling, run-down inn in South Carolina and stays there for one year. He’s out of lies and out of places to run. Now he's stuck in a falling-down house, trying to be a father to a stranger while his whole world collapses. This is AIN'T NO GOING BACK. It’s a powerful story about what happens when all the secrets finally come out.

About Corey

Corey L. Johnson’s love for storytelling began in Ripley, Tennessee, near the childhood home of literary legend Alex Haley. After years in the corporate world, Corey returned to his passion for writing. Today, he crafts emotionally resonant stories about life, love, faith, and the decisions that define us, all while living in Murfreesboro, Tennessee with his family.

Corey aims to create books that move readers and spark reflection, reminding us that even in everyday challenges there is meaning and hope.
